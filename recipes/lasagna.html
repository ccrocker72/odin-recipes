Lasagna

Description

This is the best recipe you will ever find for making lasagna. The flavor of this dish is bar none and easily the best you will ever find.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • pasta sauce
  • bechamel sauce
  • some meat
  • layers

Steps

  1. cook the pasta
  2. cook the meat
  3. layer the pasta, sauce, meat until supplies are gone
